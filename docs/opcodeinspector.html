Opcode.Inspector

FFXIV .dat packet sniffer

Drop a recording to read every server-zone IPC packet it contains — opcode, size, timing and a raw hex view of the bytes. Built to hunt down the two opcodes the workbench has to hand-enter each patch: PartyList and PartyPortraitInfo. Everything runs in your browser; the file never leaves your machine.

Both packets carry your party members' names as text, so the finder first reads the names out of the recording, then flags the opcodes whose payloads contain them. PartyPortraitInfo is the 1408‑byte (8×176) appearance packet; PartyList is the smaller roster packet. Confirm a guess by clicking it — the hex view highlights the names in the bytes.
